Overview

The Houshin Company is the largest commercial and private military entity operating within Pneuma Institute space. Founded in the aftermath of the Great Catastrophe by Noctivex merchant Souren Houshin, the Company grew from a trading operation into a sprawling conglomerate spanning commercial logistics, military contracting, intelligence brokering, and advanced manufacturing. The name Houshin is an institutional title granted on appointment to the Company's leader — it is not hereditary.

The Company operates under a posture of nominal independence while maintaining deep alignment with the Institute. This arrangement suits both parties: the Institute gains a capable, deniable PMC and intelligence network without direct institutional exposure, while the Houshin Company gains access to Institute contracts, infrastructure, and protection. The relationship is transactional at its core, managed through mutual benefit rather than loyalty.

Under Vaen Houshin — the youngest Merchant Sovereign in the Company's history — the long-term strategic goal is interstellar expansion. The Company currently operates exclusively within Institute space, but Vaen's inner circle is actively building the diplomatic and military groundwork for operations beyond it.
