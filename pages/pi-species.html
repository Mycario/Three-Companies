Biology

The Avriethrax are a compact, heavily built species adapted to Apostolos's 3g surface gravity. Despite standing only around three feet tall, individuals typically mass between 80 and 140 kilograms, with dense musculature and reinforced skeletal structure. Their bodies are covered in a scaled underside and feathery topside, with large non-functional wings that serve as display and thermoregulatory structures rather than flight apparatus — flight in Apostolos's gravity would be impossible at their mass.

They possess four eyes: two large forward-facing primary eyes and two smaller lateral compound eyes providing near-360 degree awareness. A heavy counterbalancing tail aids locomotion and posture under high gravity. Their high-G adaptation means Avriethrax in standard or low gravity environments are disproportionately strong and fast relative to their size.

Avriethrax possess natural psionic capability, the degree of which varies considerably between individuals. This manifests most commonly as heightened intuition, emotional projection, and limited telekinetic ability, though exceptional individuals — known as Focusers — can achieve far greater output under training.

Psychology & Behaviour

Avriethrax are highly social, hierarchy-oriented, and intensely territorial at the institutional level. Individual Avriethrax tend toward calculated risk-taking and long-term thinking, traits that translate naturally into the Institute's administrative and research culture. Emotional expression is typically muted in public and exaggerated in private — a cultural norm rather than a biological one.

Their psionic sense makes Avriethrax unusually perceptive of emotional states in others, which contributes to a cultural tendency toward indirect communication and layered speech. What is unsaid is often as significant as what is spoken. This makes them difficult negotiators and effective manipulators.

Society & Role Within the Institute

The Avriethrax are the founding and dominant species of the Pneuma Institute. All seven Archons are Avriethrax, and Avriethrax hold the majority of senior administrative, research, and military command positions. The Institute's governance structure, cultural norms, and religious framework all emerged from Avriethrax society predating the Great Catastrophe.

Within Apostolos, Avriethrax occupy the civilian ecumenopolis and upper administrative layers. The species' long lifespan and psionic capability — combined with access to Seraphic organ symbiosis for exceptional individuals — means effective Avriethrax leadership can persist for centuries, producing deep institutional continuity and deep institutional conservatism in equal measure.

Seraphic Symbiosis

A small number of Avriethrax — predominantly senior Archons and high-ranking Institute figures — undergo Seraphic organ symbiosis, a classified procedure in which Seraphic biological material is integrated into the host's physiology. The effects include dramatically extended lifespan, enhanced psionic output, and physiological changes that vary between recipients. The Archon of Breath is believed to be over 200 years old through this process. The full scope of symbiosis effects is known only at Archon level.

Origin & Creation

The Noctivex are not a naturally evolved species. They were engineered by the Pneuma Institute using PNE-Λ-03 — the Anabatic Mass — as a biological foundation, combined with Avriethrax genetic material. The result is a moth-insectoid humanoid species purpose-built for population density, labour output, and controllability. The Noctivex do not predate the Institute; they are its product.

Their genome is hardcoded with two critical control mechanisms: a psionic kill-switch that can be triggered remotely by Institute systems, and a submission gene that inhibits coordinated resistance behaviour at a neurological level. Neither mechanism is disclosed to the Noctivex population. Their rapid reproduction rate — any two individuals can reproduce through internal fertilisation with no biological sex distinction — was a deliberate design choice to allow fast population scaling.

Biology

Noctivex are taller and lighter than Avriethrax, with a moth and insectoid humanoid body plan. They possess four eyes — two large compound eyes providing wide-field vision and two smaller thermal-sensitive eyes that allow heat-signature detection in low light. Two hearts provide circulatory redundancy. Antennae serve chemosensory and limited psionic detection functions.

The most notable biological feature is their wing-dust — a fine particulate shed passively from their wing membranes. This dust contains prion-like compounds that are lethal to most species on contact or inhalation. Avriethrax and Noctivex themselves are immune. This was not an accidental trait. The dust provides a degree of passive deterrence but is also a limiting factor in Noctivex integration with non-Institute species.

The Noctivex are hermaphroditic by design — no individual carries a fixed biological sex. Reproduction occurs through internal fertilisation between any two individuals, without distinction. This was chosen to maximise population growth rate and eliminate sex-ratio bottlenecks.

Psychology & Behaviour

The submission gene does not produce docile or passive individuals — Noctivex are culturally vibrant, creative, and socially complex. What it suppresses is coordinated mass resistance: large-scale organised uprisings do not emerge naturally in Noctivex populations. Individual discontent, small-scale rebellion, and interpersonal conflict are entirely normal. The gene acts at the threshold of collective action, not individual will.

Noctivex society within Apostolos is densely layered, with strong communal networks, rich oral and artistic traditions, and a deep awareness — largely unspoken — of their created status. Most Noctivex do not know the specifics of the kill-switch or submission gene, but the broad shape of their situation is culturally understood and rarely discussed.

Role Within the Institute

Noctivex comprise the majority of Apostolos's labour population — manufacturing, maintenance, service, and lower administrative functions are predominantly Noctivex roles. Within the Houshin Company, Noctivex make up approximately 41% of employees. Mirror Protocol was founded and is led by a Noctivex individual, representing a rare senior position in a contractor context.

The Noctivex are not officially described as engineered or controlled within Institute public documentation. The official narrative frames them as a naturally emerging species that joined the Institute voluntarily in the post-Catastrophe period. This is false.

Nature & Classification

Seraphs are not a species in the conventional biological sense. They are massive, partially or fully sentient entities of Seraphic crystallite material — a substance not fully understood even by the Institute's senior researchers. They predate the Great Catastrophe and are foundational to the Institute's existence, technology, and theology. The Institute's theocratic framework is built around them.

Seraphs are divided into Greater and Lesser classifications. Lesser Seraphs are the source of Replicant Biological Units — the standardised combat and defence constructs grown from First Seraph remains. Greater Seraphs are fully awakened entities with independent cognition and psionic capability that dwarfs anything achievable by conventional means. The Voice of Apostolos is a minor Seraph empowered by Greater Seraphs — the distinction between minor and Greater is one of scale that is difficult to quantify.

Replicant Biological Units

RBUs are grown from First Seraph remains — biological-crystallite hybrid constructs used as the Institute's primary heavy defence and combat assets. Standard RBUs stand approximately 100 metres tall. The Apostolos planetary defenders are significantly larger, ranging from 120 to 260 kilometres in height — entities of a scale that render conventional military force effectively irrelevant in direct engagement.

RBUs are not independently intelligent in the way Greater Seraphs are. They operate under Institute direction, controlled through Focuser networks and PICS coordination. The degree to which they possess anything resembling awareness is a subject of ongoing theological and scientific debate within the Institute, conducted entirely at classification levels inaccessible to the general population.

PNE-Λ-03 — The Anabatic Mass

PNE-Λ-03 is a Seraph-adjacent proliferative biomass contained in a cryo-resonance vault 18 kilometres below Apostolos's surface at -110°C. It is the biological source material from which the Noctivex were engineered. Its precise nature — whether it constitutes a living Seraph, a Seraph remnant, a failed Seraph, or something else entirely — is unknown even to most Institute researchers. Access is restricted to Archon-authorised personnel only.

The designation "Seraph-adjacent" indicates that it shares material properties with Seraphic crystallite but does not meet the Institute's internal classification criteria for a true Seraph. Whether this distinction is meaningful or a bureaucratic convenience is unclear.

Institute Relationship

The Institute's relationship with Seraphs is its most guarded secret. Publicly, the Institute's theology positions Seraphs as divine or semi-divine entities whose remains and descendants are sacred — this framing justifies the Institute's authority, the existence of RBUs, and the reverence accorded to Focusers and Oracles. The reality is more complex: the Institute does not control Greater Seraphs, but operates under an arrangement whose nature is Archon-level classified.

The Voice of Apostolos — an 18.4km living Seraphic capital vessel — is the most visible expression of this relationship and the most classified asset the Institute possesses outside the Allar-Pneuma siege platform.
