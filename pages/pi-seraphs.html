§ 01 Containment Procedures

Seraph Prime currently exists in a collapsed state following the Great Awakening event. The remains occupy a crystalline chamber approximately 26 kilometres below the Apostolos mantle faultline, within a pressure-stable region designated the Cruciform Vault. The Vault is maintained under continuous First Choir observation via a network of 847 psionic resonance monitoring nodes embedded in the surrounding rock formations.

Access to the Cruciform Vault requires Archon-level authorisation from a minimum of three of the seven Archons. No material may be removed from the Vault without written authorisation from the Archon of Flesh and the Archon of Breath. Physical contact with any Seraph Prime remains is prohibited without Class-IV psionic suppression equipment and full biological isolation protocols.

The remains of Seraph Prime are to be considered the foundational material resource of the Pneuma Institute. Controlled harvesting of Seraphic osteoderm, crystallite lattice, and resonance-active tissue is authorised under Third Choir direction for approved research and production purposes. Uncontrolled harvesting is a Class-I offence punishable by permanent cognitive restructuring.

In the event of anomalous psionic activity originating from Seraph Prime remains exceeding threshold level 4, the Cruciform Vault is to be sealed and the Archon of Breath notified immediately. All personnel are to evacuate to minimum safe distance of 800 metres. Protocol SILENCE-PRIME is to be initiated within four minutes of threshold breach.

§ 02 Description

Seraph Prime, designated Seraph Prime, is the first Seraphic entity discovered by the Pneuma Institute and the foundational basis upon which the Institute's research, technology, and theological framework is built. It is the largest Seraphic entity currently known to the Institute, though pre-collapse dimensional estimates place its true scale as significantly greater than the physical remains presently observable.

Seraph Prime was discovered during Year 3 of the Astral Geoscience Survey, buried within a crystalline pressure-stable chamber 26 kilometres below the Apostolos mantle faultline. The AGS had been investigating a series of anomalous psionic events across the planet's surface for several years prior — mass hallucinations, geological harmonic resonance, shared dreaming events — all now understood to be passive projections from Seraph Prime in its dormant state.

On the 17th day of direct excavation, AGS researcher XXXXXXXXXX made physical contact with the entity's core structure. Seraph Prime achieved full consciousness in an event lasting approximately 0.3 seconds. The resulting energy discharge was not a directed attack but a passive consequence of consciousness activation at the entity's scale. The event vaporized a 122,000 kilometre region, imploded a continent-sized landmass into a compressed glowing sphere, buckled spacetime in the surrounding region, and triggered global catastrophe. This event is designated the Great Awakening in Institute records and the Great Catastrophe in civilian historical documentation.

Seraph Prime subsequently collapsed into its current state approximately XXXXXXXX hours after the Awakening event. The precise mechanism of collapse is not fully understood. Current leading hypothesis within the Fourth Choir posits that the entity's consciousness briefly extended into a dimensional layer inaccessible to Institute instrumentation and that what remains in the Cruciform Vault is a physical anchor rather than the totality of the entity. This hypothesis has not been confirmed or denied by the Archon of Breath.

The physical remains of Seraph Prime consist of Seraphic crystallite lattice of extraordinary density and psionic activity, osteoderm structures of a hardness exceeding any naturally occurring material known to Institute science, and several partially intact organ-analogous structures whose function remains a subject of active Third Choir research. The remains emit a continuous low-level psionic resonance consistent with residual consciousness activity. Whether this constitutes awareness is classified at Archon level.

§ 03 Research Notes

Seraph Prime remains are the source material for the Institute's entire Seraphic technology programme. Harvested crystallite lattice forms the basis of Seraphic Bore torpedo warheads, RBU armour composite, the Voice of Apostolos hull material, and the psionic resonance emitter nodes used in Forma-Vel Grid shield systems. The osteoderm structures are processed into Vel-Thraen Blade components used by MPC-WF Warframe units.

The Institute's public theological framework — which positions Seraphs as divine or semi-divine entities and frames the Institute's authority as stewardship of their remains — was constructed deliberately around Seraph Prime in the early post-Catastrophe consolidation period. The theological framing is not held internally. Seraph Prime is classified as a research and resource asset.

Note: The question of whether Seraph Prime is genuinely deceased or merely dormant in a form the Institute cannot detect remains open. All personnel interacting with Vault materials are reminded that this question is above their clearance level and is not to be discussed outside formally sanctioned research contexts.

§ 01 Containment Procedures

PNE-Λ-03 is to be maintained at or below -110°C within the Cryo-Resonance Vault located 18 kilometres below Apostolos's surface. The Cryo-Resonance Vault is a sealed chamber with continuous temperature monitoring, automated cryo-suppression systems with triple redundancy, and a psionic dampening field maintained by a rotating complement of six Focusers at all times. No single point of failure in the cryo-suppression system is acceptable — any system fault is to be treated as a containment emergency and escalated to First Choir Director level immediately.

Physical access to PNE-Λ-03 requires Archon of Flesh authorisation and a minimum of two additional Archon countersignatures. No material is to be removed from the entity without Third Choir Director oversight and a full biohazard containment team present. Personnel interacting with PNE-Λ-03 material must be tested for prion exposure within 24 hours of contact and monitored for a minimum of 30 days thereafter.

In the event of temperature exceeding -90°C for any reason, Protocol ANABATIC-HOLD is to be initiated immediately. All non-essential personnel are to evacuate the surrounding 2-kilometre zone. The Archon of Breath is to be notified within 90 seconds. Under no circumstances is the cryo-suppression system to be allowed to fail completely. The consequences of a full thermal event involving PNE-Λ-03 are classified but are considered an extinction-level containment failure by the First Choir.

§ 02 Description

PNE-Λ-03 is a proliferative biomass of indeterminate origin discovered XXXXXXXXXX years after the Great Awakening during deep subsurface survey operations conducted by the Third Choir. It shares material properties with Seraphic crystallite, exhibits biological behaviour consistent with living tissue, and responds to psionic stimuli in ways that suggest awareness. It does not, however, meet the Institute's formal classification criteria for a true Seraph. The designation "Seraph-Adjacent" reflects this ambiguity. Whether the distinction is meaningful is not resolved.

In its contained state at -110°C, PNE-Λ-03 exhibits minimal activity. Cellular-level processes continue at a greatly suppressed rate. The mass does not grow measurably at containment temperature. Psionic output is reduced to a baseline residual level that is non-hazardous with standard suppression protocols active. It does not communicate, respond to external stimuli, or exhibit any behaviour that would constitute evidence of active consciousness at containment temperature.

At temperatures above approximately -60°C, PNE-Λ-03 becomes significantly more active. Growth rate accelerates markedly. Psionic output increases. At temperatures above -20°C, the mass has demonstrated the ability to generate and shed biological material that constitutes a severe biohazard to non-immune species — a prion-analogue compound present in all shed matter that causes progressive fatal neurological degradation in any species without the specific genetic resistance profile found in Avriethrax and Noctivex populations. Notably, the Noctivex genetic immunity to this compound is a consequence of their origin: they were engineered from PNE-Λ-03 material combined with Avriethrax genetic stock, inheriting resistance from both parent sources.

PNE-Λ-03 is the biological source material from which the Noctivex species was engineered by the Third Choir. The precise nature of the entity — whether it constitutes a living Seraph, a dormant Seraph, a Seraph remnant, a failed Seraph, or something without precedent in Institute classification — is a matter of active internal debate that has not been resolved in XXXX years of research. Access to this debate's conclusions is restricted to Archon level.

§ 03 Research Notes

PNE-Λ-03 is the most significant biological resource the Institute possesses that does not derive from Seraph Prime. Its use as the foundation material for the Noctivex engineering programme represents the Institute's most ambitious application of Seraphic-adjacent biology to date, producing a species that now constitutes approximately 45% of Apostolos's total population.

Ongoing research questions include: the origin of PNE-Λ-03 and its relationship to Seraph Prime; whether the entity has kinship with any of the other Seraphic entities detected throughout the solar system; whether it is capable of the kind of awakening event exhibited by Seraph Prime; and whether the Noctivex species, as biological derivatives of PNE-Λ-03, have any form of psionic connection to it that is not detectable by current Institute instrumentation.

The last question in particular is considered sensitive. Research into this area is restricted to the Third Choir's inner directorate and requires Archon of Flesh sign-off for any new investigation. XXXXXXXXXXXXXXXXXXXXXXXXXXXXXXXXXXXXXXXXXXXXXXXXXXXXXXXXXX.
